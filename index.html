More Than An Equipment Supplier. A Partner In The Work.

Anyone can sell you a machine. Vortec starts with the application: your soils, slopes, mixes, programme and crew. We help you select the right solution, not just the right machine, so the equipment earns its keep from the first job.

Bringing erosion control in-house is about more than cutting contractor costs. It gives you control over timing, responsiveness when programmes move, consistent quality and predictable project outcomes, with the flexibility to redeploy the machine wherever the work is.

And because downtime costs more than steel, every Vortec is Australian-built, corrosion resistant and backed by genuine parts, technical support and after-sales service for the long term. That is what buying with confidence looks like.
