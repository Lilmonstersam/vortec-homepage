More Than An Equipment Supplier. A Partner In The Work.

Anyone can supply a machine. Vortec starts with the application: your soils, slopes, mixes, programme and crew. We help you select the right solution, not just the right machine, so the equipment earns its keep from the first job.

Bringing hydroseeding in-house gives you control over timing, responsiveness and the quality of hydromulch, seed and fertiliser. The flexibility to deploy your machine whenever and wherever the work is required means rework or non-compliance is no longer part of the equation.

And because downtime and erosion cost more than it's worth risking, every Vortec machine is Built Tough and backed by our Unrivalled After-Sales Service and technical support to keep you moving! Enabling you to take control and deliver projects with confidence.
